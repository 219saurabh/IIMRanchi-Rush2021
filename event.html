INFERNO (Solo Dance) *

Rules and Regulations

  • 1) Time limits: Max. 5 minutes will be provided in total.
  • 2) There will be penalty for exceeding the time limit.
  • 3) Maximum of one companion will be allowed.
  • 4) Teams are expected to carry their song on a pen drive/CD in MP3 format. Last date for confirming the song and giving the CD to the concerned SPOC is 2 hours before the start of the event.

  • 9) Any obscene/offensive music/song is not allowed.

FIREBAND (War of Bands)

Rules and Regulations

  • 1) Registration process involves sending a live jamming video of high sound quality where all the band members are visible. The video should be sent to cultural.committee@iimranchi.ac.in with subject title ‘Rush2019_FireBand_ParticipantName_CollegeName’ by 10 PM, 20th Jan’19. Mails not adhering to the above mentioned subject line will not be considered.
  • 2) No person can be in more than one band.
  • 3) 4 bands will be shortlisted based on the videos sent by them.
  • 4) Each band will be given 20 minutes on stage (Stage-in, stage-out). This includes sound check. All the teams must adhere to the time limit.
  • 5) The bands can perform original songs or covers as per their convenience.
  • 6) Use of pre-recorded sounds and textures are not allowed and any such attempt would lead to disqualification.
  • 7) Bands are required to bring their own gear. A basic 5-piece drum-kit will be provided. Bands can bring additional cymbals and their own drum pedals.
  • 8) Registration fee for this event is ₹500.
  • 9) Participants are required to reach the venue at least half an hour before the start of the event.
